👤 Meet the Founder

The journey of a visionary from Mombasa building digital solutions that transform Africa and the world.

Lesly Juma – Founder of Code MORE

Born in the coastal city of Mombasa, Kenya 🇰🇪, I’ve always believed in building with purpose. From humble beginnings to becoming a cybersecurity specialist, web developer, and visionary behind projects like Dada Flow and Coast Connect, I turned challenges into code, and code into impact.

With Code MORE, I aim to empower individuals and organizations across Africa with secure digital tools, clean web platforms, and world-class support — helping them build, break barriers, and earn more.

🔥 Our Vision & Mission

🌍 Vision

To become Africa’s leading hub of digital transformation by providing secure, scalable, and empowering tech solutions that reach the globe.

🎯 Mission

To help individuals and organizations build secure platforms, access opportunities, and thrive online through cybersecurity, development, and digital support.

🛤️ My Journey

🧒 2022 – Joined Technical University of Mombasa for IT & Computer Science but paused due to financial challenges.
💻 2023 – Studied through Cisco Networking Academy, ALX Virtual Assistant program, and started freelancing.
🛠️ 2024 – Built Lesly’s Professional Vulnerability Scanner, and launched Dada Flow Initiative for girls’ empowerment.
🚀 2025 – Started Code MORE to serve Africa and the world with secure digital solutions.
